Tender Bloom Care Work

Tender Bloom Care Work provides peer support care sessions by phone, video, or in-person, offering a compassionate, collaborative space for emotional processing. Sessions are flexible, guided by client needs, and offered on a sliding scale.

Disclaimer

I am providing lived experience peer support care work. I am not a therapist or licensed clinician. While the work we do together may resemble therapy or have a beneficial impact, it is not the same as care provided by a licensed professional counselor. The most accurate technical label for my work would be "life coaching" but I do not choose that label for myself or my practice.

I am not giving out medical advice, diagnosing conditions, or treating diseases and/or illnesses. You are the ultimate authority on your physical, mental, and spiritual needs.
